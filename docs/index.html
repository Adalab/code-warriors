Motivation the extra mile

team

We are five awesome women from different working environments, with different lifestyles and different backgrounds, but with one thing in common, we want to become front-end developers!


We dear to change our lives and become Code Warriors. Adalab has given us the opportunity to begin this journey. Where it will take us?... only the future will tell. How about you help us write a chapter of our story?

Strenghts

We know that communication and collaboration are the basis for a well-functioning team. In Code-Warriors we care about generating a good working environment, in each project we commit ourselves and the rest of the team to bring it forward by providing our best individual capabilities. We are positive and persevering, nothing can stop us!

Weaknesses

Front-end development is something new for us, which is why we strive every day to improve our technical knowledge. Beyond programming, each of us is trained in different disciplines that enrich the group and help us overcome technical difficulties. Working under pressure is also a challenge, which is why we try to ensure that all decisions are agreed by the team. Going in the same direction is the best way to reach the goal!

who we are?
